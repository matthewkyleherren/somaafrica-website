Welcome

Clyde's platform is one of a merchant's most valuable tools to drive customer lifetime value, increase margins, and enhance the overall customer experience. Our platform makes it incredibly easy to stand up a product protection program backed by top-tier companies that will immediately create value for both your business and your customers on day one.

Clyde provides all the tools you need to easily integrate our protection plan products into your website and display them throughout the purchase journey. It is a seamless way for your customers to add protection plans to their purchases.

Our documentation will ensure you're able to get your program up and running in no time and that your team is equipped to manage it (don't worry, we do all the heavy lifting). Our documents cover everything from how to integrate with our APIs to how to resolve claims.

If there's something you can't find in our documents, feel free to reach out to our team at [email protected]!
